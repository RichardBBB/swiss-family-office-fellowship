Architecture

Three layers — one purpose

Layer 01

Jurisdiction & governance

Briefings on structures, control rights, risk framing, and local co-investment norms.

Layer 02

Curated investment access

Deep dives into a limited number of vetted cases — long-form Q&A, not stage pitching.

Layer 03

Relationship & continuity

Private peer exchange and an alumni circle enabling long-term access and repeat collaboration.

Governance-first. Depth over volume.

Every session is designed to protect confidentiality and enable informed decision-making.

Program flow

Day by day

Day 0 Arrival & alignment

Private welcome dinner. Peer introductions. Confidentiality framework. Mandate alignment.

Day 1 Switzerland investment reality (family-office lens)

Legal structures (equity, convertibles, preferred), governance standards, typical term dynamics, cross-border practicalities (high level).

Day 2 Curated investment case deep dives

Six to eight cases presented as investor conversations: market logic, IP position, governance readiness, capital needs, risk disclosure. Optional 1:1 sessions.

Day 3 Institutions & strategic capital

Meet key ecosystem gatekeepers. Understand how corporate and public mechanisms interact with private capital. Transfer to the alpine setting.

Day 4 Alpine family office retreat

Closed-door FO sessions: portfolio construction, risk management in private markets, long-term optionality. Peer exchange. Alumni induction.

Day 5 Optional private extensions

Follow-up meetings, structuring clinics, selected visits (by relevance).

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this a pitching event?

No. Case sessions are structured as investor conversations with long-form Q&A. We limit volume to protect depth.

How is confidentiality handled?

Closed-door sessions under Chatham House Rule. No media, no public guest list, no recordings during case discussions. NDA can be provided for case materials.

How are cases selected?

We curate for governance readiness, IP clarity, financing logic, and relevance to the cohort's mandates.

Who attends?

An invite-only cohort of 8–12 family offices. Additional counterparties may join selected dinners or private sessions based on relevance.

What stages do you cover?

Selective early and early-growth opportunities where governance and documentation are investor-grade.

What sectors do you focus on?

Deeptech, Energy & Climate, Medtech & Life Sciences, and select B2B platforms.

What happens after the fellowship?

Where there is alignment, we facilitate selective follow-up access and introductions. Alumni may also receive private briefings.

Is an NDA available?

Yes — upon request, for case materials and follow-up discussions.

Can we bring a next-gen family member or advisor?

In many cases yes, subject to cohort size and fit.

How do we receive dates and venue options?

We share these after invitation to preserve discretion and relevance.
